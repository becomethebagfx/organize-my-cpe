# Competitive Analysis - Organize My CPE

## Market Overview

The CPE tracking market for accountants includes platform-integrated solutions and standalone trackers. Most competitors bundle tracking with their own CPE courses.

## Key Competitors

### 1. Becker CPE Compliance Tracker
- **Pricing:** $19.99/year (30-day free trial, free with Prime subscription)
- **Features:** Rules engine, compliance dashboard, certificate uploads
- **Weakness:** Primarily designed for Becker course users
- **Source:** [Becker CPE Tracker](https://www.becker.com/cpe/catalog/compliance-tracker)

### 2. NASBA CPE Audit Service
- **Pricing:** FREE
- **Features:** Basic record storage, audit compliance
- **Weakness:** Manual entry only, no AI extraction, basic UI
- **Source:** [NASBA CPE Audit Service](https://cpeauditservice.nasba.org/)

### 3. MYCPE ONE
- **Market Position:** 6M+ credits issued, 2000+ CPA firms, Top 200 firm clients
- **Features:** 15,000+ hours content, 50-state compliance, certifications
- **Weakness:** Course-focused, not upload-focused
- **Source:** [MYCPE ONE via Vocal](https://vocal.media/education/5-top-rated-online-cpe-platforms-cp-as-actually-use-in-2025)

### 4. Checkpoint Learning (Thomson Reuters)
- **Features:** Full US state coverage, Yellow Book, EA, AICPA tracking
- **Weakness:** Enterprise pricing, complex
- **Source:** [Checkpoint Learning](https://checkpointlearning.com/CPESolutions/LMS)

### 5. BeaconLive
- **Features:** All US states + Puerto Rico, multi-designation tracking
- **Weakness:** Integrated with their webinar platform
- **Source:** [BeaconLive](https://www.beaconlive.com/accounting)

### 6. Earmark CPE
- **Pricing:** Free weekly credits
- **Features:** Podcast-based CPE, mobile app, NASBA registered
- **Weakness:** Limited to Earmark-approved content
- **Source:** [Earmark CPE](https://earmarkcpe.com/)

## Unique Value Proposition

**"Upload certificates from any CPE provider. Our AI extracts course details automatically. Track compliance across all 50 states. Export in any format your state board requires."**

Key differentiators:
1. **AI-Powered Extraction:** GPT-4o-mini extracts course data from PDFs
2. **Provider Agnostic:** Works with ANY CPE certificate, not just internal courses
3. **50% Cheaper:** $9.99 vs Becker's $19.99
4. **Simple Focus:** Just tracking, no course marketplace clutter

## Target Customer Segments

1. **Solo CPAs:** Managing their own compliance across multiple states
2. **Small Firms (2-10 CPAs):** Need simple, affordable tracking
3. **Multi-State CPAs:** Licensed in 3+ states with complex requirements
4. **CFOs/Controllers:** CPAs in industry needing personal tracking
